ToREC Analysis Tool User Guide

Step 1: Enter Your Email

Provide your email address to receive the analysis results.

Step 2: Enter the RNA Sequence

Input the RNA sequence with valid characters: A, C, G, U.

Step 3: Select the Organism

Choose the organism from the dropdown: *E.coli*, *S. cerevisiae*, or *H. sapiens*.

Step 4: Choose an Optional Reporter Gene

Pick a reporter gene (e.g., GFP or mCherry) or skip this step.

Step 5: Enter a Known Trigger Sequence

If you have a known trigger, check the box and enter it. Ensure the sequence length matches the organism.

Step 6: Input the Reporter Gene Sequence

If you didn't select a reporter gene, you can enter the sequence here.

Step 7: Upload a Transcript File

Upload a file in formats like .fasta, .fa, .gb, etc., to provide transcript sequences.

Step 8: Submit the Form

After filling all fields, click "Submit" to process the form. You'll receive the results via email.
